Cancellation and Refund Policy

At Trident Luxe Styles, we ask for at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations. Please note that the deposit fee is non-refundable. You are allowed one reschedule per deposit.

Appointment Preparation

Please ensure your hair is clean, completely blow-dried, and product-free. Your appointment is only confirmed once the deposit is paid. We allow a 15-minute grace period for all appointments. Any time beyond this may result in a $15 late fee or cancellation.

Other Information

  • The deposit is paid via e-transfer, and the balance is paid in cash only.
  • Hair is provided for all services unless stated otherwise.
  • You can choose between regular square parts or freestyle C parts for single plaits.
