Cancellation and Refund Policy

We understand that life happens, and sometimes you may need to reschedule or cancel your appointment. To ensure fairness for all clients, we ask for at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations.

  • Please note the deposit fee is non-refundable.
  • You are only allowed one reschedule per deposit.

Appointment Preparation

  • Hair must be clean, completely blow-dried, and product-free.
  • Appointments are only confirmed once the deposit is paid.
  • There is a 15-minute grace period for all appointments; any time outside of this may result in the appointment being canceled or a $15 late fee applied.

Other Information

  • The deposit is paid via e-transfer, and the balance is paid in cash only.
  • Hair is provided for all services unless stated otherwise.
  • You can choose between regular square parts or freestyle C parts for single plaits.
